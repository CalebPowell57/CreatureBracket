Welcome to Creature Bracket

Creature bracket is a play on the typical march madness style bracket prediction/competition with a apples-to-apples element to it. Come up with clever/unique creature entries, make predictions as to which of the available characters will move on throughout the bracket as well as who the overall winner will be, and attempt to convince other players that your selected creatures will win in their matches.

Creature Submission

Prior to the tournament beginning, you will be defaulted to the creature submission page. As the name would suggest, here users will be able to upload an image, name, and small bio description for the creature of their choice. There are no restrictions as far as what or who can be a creature in the creature bracket. The creature can be from a comic series, game, movie, or an inanimate object, the name of the game is simply to convince others to agree/vote alongside you. Be creative and have fun with it!

Tournament Begins

The torunament will be made up of 64 user submitted creatures. Once all of the creature submissions have been approved, the torunament will begin, making the bracket avaliable for all users to access. Each round will last three days, during which all registered users will be able to cast their votes for each avaliable creature matchup. At the end of the 3 day period, the winner of each round will be determined by the system using risk rule logic.

Matchups

There are no stipulations as to which creature will be facing off against another. They could be cross-universe, cross-platform, potentially having nothing in common. This will result in interesting debates as to which creature will have an edge and move to the next round!

Voting

In order to cast your vote, simply hover over a matchup within the bracket and two boxes will appear. Click the on the box associated with the desired creature and your vote will be submitted for that matchup. You are able to switch your vote after initially casting if desired.
