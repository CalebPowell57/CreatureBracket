Creature Submission

Prior to the tournament beginning, you will be defaulted to the creature submission page. As the name would suggest, here users will be able to upload an image, name, and small bio description for the creature of their choice. Creature selection is entirely up to the user, and can be anything or anyone from Mario to a pack of raptors. Be creative and have fun with it!

Tournament Begins

The torunament will be made up of 64 user submitted creatures. Once all of the creature submissions have been approved, the torunament will begin, making the bracket avaliable for all users to access. Each round will last three days, during which all registered users will be able to cast their votes for each avaliable creature matchup. At the end of the 3 day period, the winner of each round will be determined by the system using risk rule logic.

Matchups
